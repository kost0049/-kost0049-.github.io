Programs

At Fake Public Library, we believe that learning doesn't just happen in books. That's why we offer a wide range of programs and events for all ages and interests. From storytimes for kids to book clubs for adults, we have something for everyone. We also offer workshops, classes, and special events on a variety of topics, including technology, writing, art, and more. Our programs are designed to be informative, engaging, and fun, and we are always looking for new ways to inspire curiosity and promote lifelong learning. Check out our calendar of events to see what's coming up next at Fake Public Library!
