How to Use

Find the Book: The first step in checking out a book from Fake Public Library is to find the book you want to borrow. You can use our online catalog to search for books by title, author, or keyword, or you can browse the shelves in the library to find books that interest you. Once you've found the book you want to borrow, take note of the call number and location so you can find it easily.

Check Out the Book: To check out a book, bring it to the circulation desk along with your library card. Our staff will scan your card and the book, and then stamp the due date on a slip inside the book. You can usually borrow books for up to three weeks, but the loan period may vary depending on the book and your borrowing history. Once you've checked out the book, you're free to take it home and enjoy it at your leisure. If you have any questions or need assistance with checking out a book, don't hesitate to ask our friendly staff for help!
